fasta files contains protein information used for orthologue identification for D. discoideum and D. firmibasis which are included in this example.

Orthogroups.tsv is the output from OrthoFinder (https://doi.org/10.1186/s13059-019-1832-y) and contain orthologue information for D. discoideum, D. firmibasis, D. purpureum, D. lacteum, P. pallidum, A. subglobosum and D. fasciculatum
